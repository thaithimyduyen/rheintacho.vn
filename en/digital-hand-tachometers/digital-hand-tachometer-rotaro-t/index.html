Digital hand-tachometer rotaro T

Discover precision with the Rheintacho Digital Hand-Tachometer Rotaro T—your reliable solution for accurate RPM measurement in industrial applications.

The digital hand-tachometer rotaro T is just as useful when setting up plants and machinery as during service jobs, monitoring manufacturing processes or in development laboratories. Example applications include measuring the rotational speeds of engines, pumps and turbines, as well as of mixers, centrifuges and conveyors; it can also measure the velocities and lengths of all kinds of threads, sheets and belts.

Using a special adapter, the rotaro T can measure the lenghths and velocities of yarn, wire and glass or other fiber.

Scope of delivery

A5-1200:

  • Basic device with holster
  • 10 reflective tapes
  • Adapter for mechanical measurement
  • Special mechanical adapter for threads, glass or other fiber and wire
  • Measuring tip, measuring funnel, wheels (0,1 m and 6“)
