AC-DC power pack

The RHEINTACHO power pack is characterized by its universal application and its compact design in a DIN rail housing. It has been developed for industrial use and provides the power supply for RHEINTACHO sensors.

The AC-DC power pack provides a constant output voltage of 24 VDC at 30 W and up to 1250 mA. The input voltage range is 90 to 265 VAC.

The output voltage can be adjusted up or down by 15 % using a potentiometer to suit the application.
