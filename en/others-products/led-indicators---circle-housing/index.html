Led Indicators

LED indicators - Circle housing

Applications:

The LED indicator controlled by a micro controller is specially designed for “quasi-analogue” displaying of measured data on machines and equipment with high requirements on vibration resistance.


Features:

  • Circle housing (Ø 100 mm)
  • Different signal inputs
  • Adjustable
  • Designed for high vibration resistance
