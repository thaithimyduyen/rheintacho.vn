Portable stroboscope RT STROBE pocketLED LASER

Discover the Rheintacho RT STROBE pocketLED LASER, a portable stroboscope for precise industrial measurement. Compact, reliable, and easy to use!

TWO in ONE – LED Stroboscope and Laser-Tachometer...

Within the stroboscope mode, the portable multifunction device RT STROBE pocketLED LASER flashes with up to 999,999 flashes per minute and belongs to the best-performing devices of its class. The quartz accuracy ensures absolute flash precision. 6 extremely bright LEDs ensure an exceptional illumination area.

By means of a reflective laser beam, the RT STROBE pocketLED LASER offers the possibility to determine the flash rate almost automatically and thus let moving parts appear optically “frozen”. If you only want to measure the revolutions, you can also use the stroboscope as a laser tachometer with disabled flash function.
