Portable stroboscope RT STROBE pocketLED

Discover the Rheintacho Portable Stroboscope RT STROBE pocketLED for precise industrial measurement and enhanced efficiency. Perfect for on-the-go professionals!

With up to 300,000 flashes per minute, the RT STROBE pocketLED is one of the best-performing devices in its class. In terms of light intensity, this device is second to none among portable LED stroboscopes world-wide.

The quartz accuracy of the control function ensures absolute flash precision. The objectives of "maximum light yield" and "homogenous surface illumination" were ideally implemented in the development of the optical assembly.

Cutting-edge, ultra-high performance LEDs and intelligent control electronics play a key role in achieving this.
