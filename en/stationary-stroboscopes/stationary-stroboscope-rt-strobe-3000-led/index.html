Stationary stroboscope RT STROBE 3000 LED

Due to the configuration with 80 high-performance LEDs, the RT STROBE 3000 LED offers a powerful light intensity.

The large, homogeneous illumination surface delivers superb results even at difficult light conditions, making the RT STROBE 3000 LED suitable for a very wide range of measurement, observation and inspection functions. It is especially suitable for demanding applications, such as those in the printing industry.

Technical Data

Frequency range 0 ... 120,000 FPM
Light intensity max. 6,000 LUX @ 300 mm
Illumination area 200 x 300 mm
