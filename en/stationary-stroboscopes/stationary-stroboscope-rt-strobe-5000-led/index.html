Stationary stroboscope RT STROBE 5000 LED

Discover precision with the Rheintacho RT STROBE 5000 LED, your ultimate stationary stroboscope for accurate industrial measurements.

Due to the configuration with 120 high-performance LEDs, the RT STROBE 5000 LED offers a powerful light intensity.

The large, homogeneous illumination surface delivers superb results even at difficult light conditions,making the RT STROBE 5000 LED suitable for a very wide range of measurement, observation and inspection functions. It is especially suitable for demanding applications, such as those in the printing industry.
