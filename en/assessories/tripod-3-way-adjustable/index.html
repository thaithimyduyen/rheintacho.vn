Tripod, 3-way adjustable

The tripod's lightweight design is ideal for use with stationary stroboscopes. The tripod extends up to 1 m and provides 180-degree panning for the perfect angle.

This compact, lightweight and portable tripod is ideal for a variety of applications.

Suitable for all stationary stroboscopes as well as for portable stroboscopes RT STROBE pocketLED (in connection with mounting kit for tripod or articulating arm), RT STROBE qbLED and RT STROBE super qbLED.


Features:

  • Extendable legs reach 1 m (26 cm when retracted)
  • Rotating / Tilting ball-Joint Mount with thumb screw lock
  • Part-Number: N0001.393
