RT STROBE control

RT STROBE control is your easy-to-handle control centre for any stroboscope, whether it is from RHEINTACHO or another manufacturer. Use it to create a trigger signal or to influence a signal generated externally. The multiplicity of control parameters make it easy for you to carry out the inspection or measurement tasks required of you
